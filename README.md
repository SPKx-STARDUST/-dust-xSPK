# 🐾 Bosley's Pet Nutrition Guide

A fast and easy nutrition reference tool for Bosley's team members and pet parents.

## 📖 About

Bosley's Pet Nutrition Guide is designed to simplify pet food recommendations by providing quick access to nutritional information for both dogs and cats.

Instead of searching through paperwork, staff can instantly find:

- Dog food brands and ratings
- Cat food brands and ratings
- Protein percentages
- Fat percentages
- Weight management options
- Product comparisons
- Nutritional insights

## 🚀 Features

✅ Fast food brand lookup

✅ Dog & Cat nutrition database

✅ Easy-to-read percentage ratings

✅ Mobile-friendly interface

✅ Organized by nutrition categories

✅ Helps staff make informed recommendations

## 🐕 Dog Nutrition

Browse and compare dog food brands based on nutritional quality, protein levels, fat content, and overall ratings.

## 🐈 Cat Nutrition

Access cat food nutritional information with clear rankings and easy comparison between brands.

## 🎯 Purpose

This project was created to help Bosley's employees quickly access pet nutrition information and reduce the time spent searching through printed guides and paperwork.

The goal is simple:

> Deliver accurate pet nutrition information in seconds.

## 💡 Why This App?

- Saves time for employees
- Improves customer service
- Makes nutrition recommendations easier
- Keeps important information organized in one place
